Programs

We Offer Digital Literacy Programs.


Digital Literacy for Primary Schools.
Digital literacy for primary schools involves Supporting Teachers in ICT integration and Providing schools with Digital Content.
We teach young students basic digital skills, internet safety, information evaluation, digital communication, coding basics, and fostering good online behavior.
It's vital for preparing students to navigate the digital world and become responsible digital citizens.

Digital Literacy for Youths & Women.
On the Digital literacy for youths and women we offer free trainings on ICT skills,innovation and emerging Technologies. As Microsoft Certified Trainers we train on;
.Introduction to Computer,
.Microsoft365
.Cyber Security,
.Online Jobs.

Digital Literacy for ECDE Centres.
Digital literacy for ECDE centers aims in Developing Children's skills in the use of images and sounds to convey information, ideas and feelings about themselves, their activities and environment through use of electronic media.
We strike to a balance between early exposure to technology and maintaining the development of critical physical, social, and cognitive skills needed during the early childhood years.
It lays the groundwork for future digital competence while respecting the unique needs of young children.
