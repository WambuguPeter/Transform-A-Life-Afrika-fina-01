Our Partners

Ministry of Education - Kenya

The organization collaborates with the Ministry of Education to integrate digital empowerment programs into the formal education system, ensuring that children, youths, and women have access to quality digital education resources.
This partnership aims to equip the next generation with essential digital skills to foster Sustainable Development in Africa.

Laikipia County Government

Through collaboration with county governments, the organization works to create localized, community-driven digital empowerment initiatives.
These initiatives address the specific needs of Laikipia and support the empowerment of marginalized groups, fostering inclusivity and driving Sustainable Development at the grassroots level.

African Storybook Organization.

Partnering with the African StoryBook Organization bolsters the mission of transforming the lives of children, youths, and women through strategic digital empowerment.
This collaboration brings a wealth of educational resources, particularly in storytelling and literacy, enhancing the organization's efforts. Together, they work to provide access to a diverse range of digital educational content, supporting Sustainable Development in Africa.
The partnership embodies the core values of collaboration and innovation, promoting excellence in educational content while maintaining the integrity of their shared vision of empowered individuals and a better Africa.

Concordia University.

The collaboration with Concordia University ensures academic rigor and excellence in the organization's programs.
Concordia's involvement bolsters the quality and impact of digital empowerment initiatives, setting a high standard for educational content and delivery.

Ubongo International.

The organization leverages their expertise in edutainment and digital media to deliver engaging and educational content to children, youths, and women.
This collaboration aims to make learning fun and accessible, furthering the goal of empowerment through technology.

WHO WE ARE

Transform a Life Afrika is a nonprofit Organization found on 2022. We are dedicated in empowering young people by equipping them with essential digital skills, fostering their personal and professional development, and thereby enhancing their opportunities for a brighter and more prosperous future.
